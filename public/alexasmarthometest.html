Troubleshooting Tips

Common reasons why your Alexa app might not be finding devices:

  • Lambda Function Permissions - Ensure your Lambda has permission to invoke your Railway server
  • CORS Issues - Your Railway server must allow requests from Alexa services
  • OAuth Configuration - Verify your OAuth setup in the Alexa Developer Console
  • Skill Configuration - Ensure your skill is properly linked to your Lambda function
  • SSL Certificate - Your Railway server must have a valid SSL certificate
  • Endpoint Verification - Alexa verifies endpoints, ensure your server responds correctly
